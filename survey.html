IRIS is a simple, single-use, skin-mounted needle guide designed to streamline CT-guided biopsies by reducing confirmation scans, shortening procedure time, and improving targeting—without capital equipment or workflow disruption. This page gives you a quick overview, followed by a short 3-minute survey.

What it is

Disposable, skin-mounted needle guide for CT-guided procedures; intuitive “protractor-like” angle control at the skin entry site.

What it does

Helps achieve the planned entry angle & depth with fewer confirmation scans—targeting accuracy with less iteration.

Why it matters

Potential to reduce radiation exposure, shorten procedure time, and increase daily case throughput—without capital spend.
